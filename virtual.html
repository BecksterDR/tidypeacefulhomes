What To Expect

  • Each session is a 60 minute video chat via Google Meet which you can join on your phone, tablet, or computer
  • Be sure to have questions, concerns, and topics prepared before the session to make the best use of our time
  • I will provide personalized tips and support for decluttering and organizing
  • I will teach you long-term habits to maintain your space confidently
  • We can work on any area of your home, including closets, kitchens, garages, and more
  • After the session, I will send you a summary of our discussion and any additional resources or tips
  • Follow-up sessions can be scheduled as needed to continue your progress
